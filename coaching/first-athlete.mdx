# Invite your first athlete

Your roster starts empty. Here's how to fill it — one athlete, a paste of many, or a CSV.

## Invite one athlete

From your roster, click **Invite** → **One athlete**. Enter their email (and optionally their name, which personalizes the invite). They get a branded email with a link to accept.

When they accept, they choose to share their Saturday data with you, and they appear on your roster. If they don't have a Saturday account yet, the invite walks them through creating one.

<Note>
  **Consent is built in.** An athlete always explicitly accepts coaching and agrees to share their data. You never silently gain access to someone's account.
</Note>

## Invite many at once

Click **Invite** → **Many (paste or CSV)**. This is something most coaching tools can't do.

- **Paste tab:** drop in a block of emails — commas, spaces, or one per line, mixed however. Saturday sorts them out as you type: valid emails turn green, duplicates collapse, typos get flagged for a quick fix, and anyone already on your roster is skipped. A running count shows "42 valid · 3 duplicates · 1 invalid."
- **CSV tab:** drag in a `.csv`. Saturday auto-detects the email column (and an optional name column to personalize each invite). If it's ambiguous, you pick the column.

Before sending, you'll see the **seat impact** — for example, "Inviting 42 athletes. Your plan includes 5 seats; the rest will be self-pay unless you cover them." Invites never auto-charge you; you decide coverage per athlete after they accept.

## Create / set up an athlete for them

Coaching a less-techy athlete? You can **set up their fueling on their behalf** — pre-fill their [Setup](/coaching/nutrition/setup-dials) (bottles, mix, ratio) before they ever open the app — then hand off a one-tap "claim your account" invite. They arrive to a working configuration instead of a blank slate.

## Manage pending invites

| Action | What it does |
|--------|--------------|
| **Resend** | Re-send an invite that hasn't been accepted yet |
| **Cancel / revoke** | Withdraw an invite before it's accepted |
| **Transfer** | Hand off an athlete to (or from) another coach — the athlete consents |

## After they accept

Once an athlete is on your roster you can:

- See their fueling history, adherence, and flags — [Flags & groups](/coaching/roster/flags-and-groups).
- Read their AI fueling report — [AI report](/coaching/roster/ai-report).
- View and adjust their **Setup** — [The Setup](/coaching/nutrition/setup-dials).
- Decide who pays for their Saturday subscription — [Who pays](/coaching/billing/who-pays).
- Message them or add them to a group.

<Tip>
  **The fastest first win:** invite an athlete, open their AI report, and use "Apply this plan to their Setup." You've delivered value before your first conversation.
</Tip>
