# Bottling — fill & mix

<div id="bottling-fill-mix" />

**What it is.** An athlete's **Setup** describes the bottles and flasks they carry — their **carriage** — and how each one is filled and mixed. Saturday maps the per-hour prescription onto that carriage so the athlete knows exactly what to put in each bottle and when to drink it. That's the whole journey: **carriage → consumption.**

**When to turn it.** When the athlete's gear changes (more or fewer bottles, a bigger flask, a feed zone on course), when a too-concentrated mix is causing stomach trouble, or when what they carry doesn't cover the session's duration.

**What changes for the athlete.** A concrete bottle plan — fill volumes, scoop counts, and a drink cadence — with none of the math left to them. They just drink the plan.

**The question it answers.** *"How much do I actually put in each bottle, and how often do I drink?"*

## What you do as a coach

- **Read it first.** Open the athlete's current carriage on athlete-detail before changing anything — see ground truth, then adjust.
- **Adjust the count and concentration.** In [Adjust Setup](/coaching/nutrition/setup-dials), change how many bottles they carry and how concentrated each is. Saturday re-maps the consumption plan and shows a live impact sentence.
- **Pre-fill for a new athlete.** Setting up a less-techy athlete? Dial in their bottling before they ever open the app — they arrive to a working plan (assisted onboarding).

<Note>
  **Always "bottle."** Saturday says bottle — in the app, the portal, and these docs. Same word everywhere so nothing gets lost in translation with your athletes.
</Note>
