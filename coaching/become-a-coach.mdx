# Become a coach

There are two doors into coaching. Both end in exactly the same state: a coach account with portal access, your own athlete subscription intact, an empty roster, and a short activation checklist to get you going.

## The two entry paths

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="From the Saturday app" icon="mobile">
    Already a Saturday athlete? Open **Settings → Become a Coach** (or tap the prompt on the home screen). Your existing account becomes a coach account — nothing about your own training changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="From the coach portal" icon="browser">
    No Saturday account yet? Sign up at [coach.saturday.fit](https://coach.saturday.fit). We provision an athlete account for you automatically so your coach identity and athlete identity are one account from day one.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  **Both paths converge.** Whichever door you use, you land at the same end state: `is_coach` on your account, app **and** portal access, your own athlete subscription, an empty roster, and an activation checklist. No second account to manage.
</Note>

## Every coach is also an athlete

This is an invariant, not a quirk: **every coach has a Saturday athlete account and completes athlete onboarding.** Why — because you coach fueling best when you've felt it yourself, and because the persona toggle lets you flip between coaching and your own training without logging out.

- **Portal-first sign-up:** we auto-create your athlete account and subscription; you can finish athlete onboarding in-app whenever you like.
- **Already have an app account:** we keep it. Same login, now with coaching powers.
- **Signed up twice (app identity ≠ portal identity):** we detect the mismatch and guide you to link/merge the two into one account. This is the steered default — you almost never want two.
- **Genuinely want them separate?** That's supported for power users, but the merge path is the recommended one.

## Your activation checklist

Your empty dashboard shows a dismissible checklist so you're never staring at a blank screen:

1. **Invite your first athlete** — see [Invite your first athlete](/coaching/first-athlete).
2. **Set your billing** — choose your plan and decide who pays. See [Who pays](/coaching/billing/who-pays).
3. **Explore the coaching guide** — the nutrition curriculum (these docs). Start at [the dials](/coaching/nutrition/overview).

Dismiss it any time; it won't nag.

## Choosing a plan

You don't have to pay to start. The plans:

| Plan | Price | Included athletes | Assistants |
|------|-------|-------------------|------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 1 | — |
| **Pro Coach** | $12.99/mo | 2 | +1 |
| **Head Coach** | $49.99/mo | 5 | +5 |
| **Business** | $149/mo flat | fair-use | unlimited |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Custom |

<Tip>
  **Saturday Lifetime owner?** Your coaching plan is **50% off, forever** — Pro Coach is $6.50/mo, Head Coach is $25.00/mo. The discount applies automatically; you'll see it on the plan cards.
</Tip>

Plans are monthly (no annual coach plans). You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel any time — see [Take over & relinquish](/coaching/billing/take-over-relinquish) for what happens to your athletes when your plan changes.

## What "access revoked" looks like

If your coach access is ever revoked (for example, your organization removes you), the portal tells you clearly what happened and what to do next — it never just shows a blank or broken screen.
